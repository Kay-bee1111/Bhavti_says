न होकर भी सदा रहूं, कुछ यूं संसार में रमना है ।

हर श्रोता के कानों में ,हर पाठक के हृदय में बसना है।

न पाखंड , न आडंबर ,शब्दों से अमृत बनाती हूं ,
हर बात सच्चे मन से कहूं ,तभी ' भवति ' कहलाती हूं ।

Bhavti | भवति

About Me

Poetess | Dreamer | Word Weaver

Welcome to my world of words and wonder. I am Bhavti, a poetess whose soul speaks through verses, weaving emotions into the fabric of poetry. For years, I have found solace in stanzas, expressing the deepest hues of my heart and the profound echoes of existence.

My journey with words began in childhood, nurtured by an insatiable love for books. Initially, I poured my thoughts into English, but over time, I discovered that Hindi, my mother tongue, carried a deeper sense of belonging. It allowed me to forge a stronger connection with my audience, making my poetry not just an expression, but a shared experience.

First Publication

My first poem was published in "The Literary Echo" when I was just sixteen, after being written on the back of a café napkin during a summer rainstorm.

Inspiration

I draw inspiration from dawn walks by the ocean, conversations with strangers, and the complex emotions that often hide between spoken words.

Writing Ritual

Each morning begins with a cup of lavender tea, three minutes of meditation, and writing one verse before reading anything else.
